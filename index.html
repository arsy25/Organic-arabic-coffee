Organic Arabic Coffee

Organic Arabic Coffee is naturally cultivated using agroforestry methods in the tropical forest conservation area of Mount Kareumbi Masigit, West Java. Located at an altitude of over 1,209 meters above sea level, this area offers a rich ecosystem that supports the growth of high-quality coffee with a distinctive mountain flavor. Grown around the forest with sustainable principles, this coffee not only delivers exceptional taste but also contributes to environmental preservation and supports local communities. We are committed to providing the finest organic arabica coffee that is environmentally friendly and rooted in conservation values.

ORGANIC ARABIC COFFEE

Our Organic Arabic Coffee is cultivated organically in the conservation forest area of Mount Kareumbi Masigit, West Java, at an elevation of over 1,209 meters above sea level. It produces a coffee with a distinctive mountain profile — balanced, complex, and rich in aroma.
